Seed Mail is a mobile phone application with cloud service which developed in Beijing, China, by Mangrove, Inc. Seed Mail will help you receiving and sending email, manage contacts and calendar in mobile devices. In order to provide these services, we need access and store some of related data.

Email
When you add an email account in Seed Mail, you will give Seed Mail permission to securely access relate data with this account. Seed Mail will only store your accounts and password in your mobile device to help you receiving and sending emails and securely store it on server in order to send push notifications , when you have new messages. We will not use these information for any purpose other than to provide Seed Mail services to you.

Contacts & Calendar
Seed Mail provides the ability to manage your contacts and calendar, before you give the access to your account information. We will not use this information for any purpose other than to provide Seed Mail service to you.

Local storage
Seed Mail stores information on your device, like your most recently email, contacts and event.Changing or deleting your information When you delete your email account, all cached and stored data of Seed Mail will remove both data on your device and server.

Link to other services or sites
Email that you received from others may have some 3rd-party website links, you agree that Seed Mail are not responsible for them.

Newsletters
When you add accounts in Seed Mail, you agree that Seed Mail can send our newsletter to your accounts at same time. In compliance with US CAN-SPAM laws, you can unsubscribe at anytime.
