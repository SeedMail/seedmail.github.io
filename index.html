Simplicity & efficiency email app

Seed Mail is design for making your work easier on mobile device, not only can you handle email efficiently by it, but also can manage contacts and calendar in one app. Seed Mail will be the best companion of your mobile work.

Seed Mail supports all Exchange and IMAP accounts:
Gmail\Google Apps, Outlook, Hotmail, Yahoo, QQ, 163, and any IMAP/Exchange account.
